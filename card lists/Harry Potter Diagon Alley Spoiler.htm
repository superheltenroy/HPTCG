Diagon Alley Set Card Spoilers - 3/13/02
Updated: 7/18/02

Power Types have coded references (rather than the symbols printed on the card). Power will be referenced as: F (Care of Magical Creatures), C (Charms), P (Potions), Q (Quidditch), and T (Transfiguration). The number before the type designates how much power is required in order to play the card. (e.g. 3C means you need three power, with at least one of that power being Charms power.)

Flavor text has NOT been included. Character and Adventure cards require 2 actions to be played, and all other cards use up only 1 action (unless another card says otherwise).

Rare cards are printed in holo/foil and non-holo/foil and both counterparts share the same card number for the card.
