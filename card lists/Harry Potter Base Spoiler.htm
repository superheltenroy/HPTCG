Abbreviations for Spell Cost:
 


P= Potions Lesson


T= Transfiguration Lesson


F = Care of Magical Creatures Lesson<


C = Charms Lesson
 
--------------
 
Spell cost = This spoiler was created with abbreviations for Spell cost.
 
Example:  Card #21 is “Delivery Owl”.  The cost to cast this creature spell 
is “2F” on our spoiler.  Which means you only need two lessons in play, 
but one has to be F (Care of Magical Creatures). You may find an 
easier/better way to abbreviate this in the future, but that’s how 
I decided to do it for this spoiler.  
 
--------------
 
Books quotes from the bottom of the card have NOT been included. 

        
 

Harry Potter Spoiler

Base Set

 

1 Dean Thomas

Holo Character

Wizard/Gryffindor/Unique

Once per game, draw 3 cards.

 

2 Draco Malfoy

Holo Character

Wizard/Slytherin/Unique

During your turn, you may use an action and discard a card from your hand to look at your opponent's hand. You may then choose one card in his or her hand and discard it.

 

3 Draco Malfoy

Foil Character

Wizard/Slytherin/Unique

During your turn, you may use an action and discard a card from your hand to look at your opponent's hand. You may then choose one card in his or her hand and discard it.

 

4 Dragon's Escape

Foil Adventure

Effect: Before each of your turns, your opponent takes 1 damage.

To Solve: Your opponent chooses 3 of his or her Lessons in play and discard them.

Opponent's Reward: Your opponent draws 3 cards or does 3 damage to you.

 

5 Elixir of Life

Foil Spell/Healing

10P

To play this card, discard 2 of your P Lessons from play. Shuffle up to 16 Non-Healing cards from your discard pile into your deck.

 

6 Gringotts' Cart Ride

Foil Adventure

Effect: Before each of your opponent's turns, he or she chooses a card in his or her hand (if any) and discards it.

To Solve: Your opponent skips a total of 5 Actions. (Those Actions don't have to be one right after the other.)

Opponent's Reward: Your opponent may draw 5 cards.

 

7 Hannah Abbott

Holo Character

Witch/Hufflepuff/Unique

Once per game, you may trade 2 cards in your hand for 2 non-Healing cards in your discard pile.

 

8 Harry Potter

Holo Character

Wizard/Gryffindor/Unique

Whenever you use an Action to draw a card, you may draw 2 cards instead of 1.

 

9 Hermione Granger

10 Hermione Granger

To Solve: Your opponent chooses and discards 1 of his or her Characters in play (other than his or her starting character) or 8 cards from their hand.

Opponent's Reward: Your opponent may draw 3 cards.
